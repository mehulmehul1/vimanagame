# Level: Music Room (Aquatic)

## Level Purpose
This level represents **music** within an organic alien ship environment, combined with **water, nature, and alien technology**.  
The level focuses on sound cues, timing-based interaction, and visual guidance.

---

## Player Start

- Player spawns inside a **dark cave**.
- Player movement is enabled immediately.
- No enemies or hazards present.

---

## Section 1: Cave (Start Area)

### Environment
- Narrow cave tunnel.
- Ceiling has **bio-luminescent growth** providing low-intensity light.
- **Stalactite formations** on the ceiling.
- Water drips from stalactites to the ground.
- Cave walls contain **glowing horizontal lines**.
- Floor is **wet and muddy**.

### Audio
- Continuous dripping water sound.
- Each drip produces a **soft chime-like echo**.
- Player footsteps create **splashing sounds**.

### Gameplay
- Traversal only.
- Player walks forward through the tunnel.

---

## Section 2: Cave Exit Transition

- Player exits the cave into a large open space.
- A **brief flash of light** occurs to emphasize scale change.
- Ground material transitions from mud to sand.

### Audio Changes
- Footstep sound switches from splashing to **coarse sand movement**.

---

## Section 3: Dome Interior (Main Area)

### Environment
- Large enclosed dome.
- Front area is a **sandy beach**.
- Footprints appear briefly when walking on sand.
- A **large body of water** occupies most of the dome.
- Water moves gently and creates small waves that reach the sand.

### Ceiling
- Three **circular transparent windows** in the ceiling.
- Windows have an **organic membrane-like coating**.
- Soft white light enters through the windows.

### Structures
- **Curved floating rock formations** inside the water.
- **Large tree-like organic structures** grow from the water.
- These structures connect to pillars and merge with the ceiling.

---

## Section 4: Platform and Harp

### Platform
- Rock platform located at the front-center of the beach.
- Slightly elevated above sand.
- Focused light shines down on the platform.

### Harp
- Organic harp placed on the platform.
- Harp has **6 strings**.
- Harp is stationary.

### Interaction
- Harp highlights when player approaches.
- Interaction prompt appears: `Press E`.

---

## Section 5: Harp Interaction Mode

### Enter Interaction
- Player presses `E`.
- Camera locks onto the harp.
- Player movement is disabled.
- Lower 1/3 of screen displays harp interface.

### Exit Interaction
- Player can press `E` again to exit.
- Player must complete harp interaction to progress.

---

## Section 6: Music Puzzle Mechanics

### Visual Cue System
- **Jelly-like creatures** jump out of the water.
- Each jump creates water ripples.
- Ripples move toward specific harp strings.
- Ripples indicate the order of strings to play.

### Puzzle Rules
- Each sequence has **3 steps**.
- Jelly creatures jump every **5 seconds**.
- Incorrect input resets the sequence.
- Correct input progresses the level.

---

## Section 7: Vortex Formation

### First Success
- A vortex begins forming at the far end of the dome.
- Camera focuses briefly on the vortex once, then returns control.

### Progression
- Total required sequences: **3**.
- Each sequence:
  - Uses a different pattern.
  - Slightly increases in difficulty.
  - Remains simple and readable.

- With each completed sequence:
  - Vortex becomes more defined.

---

## Section 8: Platform Movement

- After third successful sequence:
  - Harp becomes inactive.
  - Camera locks briefly.
  - Platform detaches from the beach.
  - Platform moves toward the vortex.
  - Sand is left behind.

- Player remains on the platform.

---

## Section 9: Vortex Interaction

- Player regains control inside the vortex.
- Vortex displays shifting colors and light patterns.
- Beams of light float above water.
- Beams indicate final harp input.

### Final Input
- Player plays final tune.
- Vortex collapses.

---

## Section 10: Transition Scene

- Water surrounds the player.
- Bubbles rise.
- Scene is non-hostile.
- Player is carried forward.

---

## Section 11: White Room (End Area)

### Environment
- Quiet white room.
- Platform emerges from water.
- Single white door ahead.
- Stairs lead up to the door.

### Collectible
- A shell floats out of the water in front of the player.
- Shell highlights on hover.
- Interaction prompt appears: `Press E`.

### Progression Lock
- Invisible wall blocks exit until shell is collected.
- Wall disappears after pickup.

---

## Level Completion

- Player walks up stairs.
- Player exits through the door.
- Level ends.
- Shell is stored in inventory.
